Sales & Profit Dashboard
This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze Sales, Profit, Customer Trends, and Category Performance.
The dashboard helps users explore business performance across years, product categories, customers, and states using dynamic visuals and slicers.

📈 Dashboard Visuals
1. Profit by Year
A clustered column chart comparing yearly profit across:
•	Furniture
•	Office Supplies
•	Technology
Helps track profitability trends across four years (2021–2024).
________________________________________
2. Sales by Category
A funnel chart showing sales distribution across multiple product types such as:
•	Phones
•	Chairs
•	Storage
•	Binders
•	Tables
… and more.
This visual highlights top-grossing product categories.
________________________________________
3. Customer Count by Year
Displays total customers for each year, enabling quick comparison of customer growth or decline.
________________________________________
4. Sales by State (Map Visual)
A geographical heat map showing:
•	High and low sales by U.S. state
•	Darker colors represent higher sales values
________________________________________
5. Top 5 Customers Profit
A bar chart showing the most profitable customers including:
•	Tamara Chand
•	Raymond Buch
•	Sanjit Chand
•	Adrian Barton
•	Martinez
Useful for identifying your top value clients.
________________________________________
6. Sales by Month
A line chart displaying monthly sales trends, including:
•	Seasonal increases/decreases
•	Monthly peaks (e.g., September & December)
________________________________________
🔍 Interactive Filters (Slicers)
Users can dynamically filter the dashboard using:
•	Category (Furniture / Office Supplies / Technology)
•	Years (2021–2024)
•	Months (Jan–Dec)
These slicers allow customizable exploration of the data.
